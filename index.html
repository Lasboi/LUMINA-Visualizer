LUMINA - Immersive Audio

How to connect your music:

  1. Start playing some music in another tab (like YouTube or Spotify) or on your desktop.
  2. Click the "Connect Audio" button below.
  3. A browser prompt will appear. Select the specific tab or screen where your music is playing.
  4. Crucial Step: Make sure to toggle "Also share tab audio" or "Share system audio" before confirming!
  5. Click "Share" and enjoy the visualizer.

Select a Live Radio Stream:

Mobile browsers block external app audio, so pick a live stream below to see the visualizer react instantly!
